Vector of returns: $ \mu \in \mathbb{R}^{n}$

Covariance matrix: $\Sigma \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n}$

Return to achieve: $r \in \mathbb{R}$

$\text{min}_{x \in [0,1]^n}\ x^{T} \Sigma x$

given

$ \Sigma_{i = 1}^{n} x_i = 1$

$ \Sigma_{i = 1}^{n} x_i\mu_i = r$
